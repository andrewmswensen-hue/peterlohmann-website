About Me

About Peter Lohmann

An electrical engineer who fell into property management, I bring a systems-first mindset to an industry full of operational chaos, and I help other operators do the same.

I run three businesses in the property management world. I'm the CEO of RL Property Management, a 740+ unit residential company in Columbus, Ohio; the owner of Peter Lohmann Media; and a co-founder of Crane, an exclusive online community for PM owners and operators.

Through Peter Lohmann Media, I serve the broader residential property management industry. My twice-weekly newsletter reaches over 20,000 professionals with actionable insights on what's working (and what's not) inside real property management companies. I host a podcast, now in its sixth season, where I interview business owners about growth, hiring, systems, and the realities of leadership. And my annual research (the PM Trends Report, Property Management M&A Report, and Largest PM Companies List) gives operators better benchmarks and clearer decisions.

I live in a suburb of Columbus, where I enjoy hiking, reading, relaxing in the sauna, making dope memes, and above all, spending time with my wife, our two daughters, and our dog Oxley.
