The Largest Property Management Companies

For the last few years, I've published a self-reported list of the 20 largest residential property management companies in the U.S. This page is the permanent home for that list.

You'll always find the most current yearly rankings here, along with past years for comparison. The list covers SFR and small multifamily (under 100 units). No HOAs, no big multifamily, no mixed portfolios. It's built to show what real scale looks like, and how top operators structure their teams, tech, and systems.

Why It Matters

Benchmarks for a fragmented industry.

Property management is famously fragmented. Whether you manage 200 doors or 20,000, seeing how the largest operators are built gives you a real benchmark to measure against, and a map of where the ceiling actually is.

Methodology

  • Only third-party managed doors are counted
  • Figures are self-reported
  • Covers SFR and small multifamily only (under 100 units)
  • Data is updated annually

What counts as "large"?

SFR management is operationally complex, so passing 5,000+ third-party doors represents a serious achievement in team, technology, and systems.
